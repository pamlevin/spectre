    Forward SNMP Traps and Syslog Messages

    1. Click the Settings icon ( ), and then select Network Monitoring > Forwarding.
    2. Click the Filters tab at the top of the page, and then click the Add button to add filters.
      Figure 14  Filters page
    3. Click the Destinations tab at the top of the page, and then click the Add button to add forwarding destinations.
      If you want the same destination for both syslogs and SNMP traps, you must add that destination twice, once for syslogs and once for traps.
    4. Click the Credentials tab at the top of the page to add SNMPv3 credentials of the receiver who is allowed to receive the forwarded traps.
